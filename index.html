Step 1 — Account setup

Connect your TikTok account

To enable automatic publishing, connect your TikTok account using the button on the right. You'll be taken to TikTok to log in and approve access — then redirected straight back here.

  • 🎵
    Publish videos to your account The app posts your original music video clips to your TikTok profile on your schedule — as many times a day as you want.
  • 🔒
    Verify your identity Used to confirm which TikTok account you're connecting. No personal data is stored or shared.
  • 🚫
    What we don't access We never read your messages, follower list, analytics, or any data beyond what's needed to post your content.

You can disconnect at any time from TikTok Settings → Security → Authorised Apps.
