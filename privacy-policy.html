Daphnie App Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 | Effective date: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Daphnie App collects, uses, stores, and protects information obtained through your use of our music content distribution platform and its integration with the TikTok API.

By using Daphnie App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to Daphnie App, we collect the following through the TikTok API:

  • Basic account information — via the user.info.basic scope: your TikTok user ID and display name, used solely to identify and authenticate your account.
  • Content publishing data — via the video.publish and video.upload scopes: metadata associated with video uploads, used solely to confirm successful delivery of your content to TikTok.

We do not collect passwords, financial information, private messages, follower lists, or any data beyond what is necessary to provide the content scheduling and publishing service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and link your TikTok account to the platform
  • To publish video content to your TikTok account at scheduled times on your behalf
  • To verify successful publication and update your content schedule accordingly
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the platform

3. Data Storage and Security

OAuth access tokens are stored locally on your device in an encrypted configuration file and are not transmitted to any third-party server operated by us. Video files are processed locally and temporarily uploaded to a secure cloud storage service (Dropbox) for the sole purpose of transferring them to TikTok, then deleted immediately after upload is confirmed.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • TikTok — video content and metadata are transmitted to TikTok via their Content Posting API as part of the core service. TikTok's Privacy Policy applies: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Dropbox — used as a temporary transfer intermediary. Files are not retained. Dropbox's Privacy Policy applies: dropbox.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

  • OAuth tokens are retained on your device until you disconnect your account or revoke access
  • Video files are deleted from temporary storage immediately after successful upload
  • Upload logs are retained for up to 30 days then deleted automatically

6. Your Rights

  • Access — you may request a copy of any personal data we hold
  • Deletion — disconnect your TikTok account to delete your data
  • Revocation — revoke access at any time via TikTok Settings → Security → Authorised Apps

7. Children's Privacy

Daphnie App is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
